About Interbus

INTERBUS is the largest ticketing service provider for Inter-City bus transportation, serving more than 10 transport providers covering nearly 40 destinations with about 425 schedules daily across Harare. Its unique features enhanced the operational efficiency and performance for the transport providers and streamlined the travel tickets booking across industry. INTERBUS is one of the most recognized and reputed brands in Harare, Zimbabwe.
