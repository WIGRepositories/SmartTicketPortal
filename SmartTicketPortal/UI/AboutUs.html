ABOUT US

INTERBUS is the largest ticketing service provider for Inter-City bus transportation, serving more than 10 transport providers covering nearly 40 destinations with about 425 schedules daily across Harare. INTERBUS became an icon providing efficient and hassle free transport ticket booking srevices to passengers and ticketing management services to transport providers. Its unique features enhanced the operational efficiency and performance for the transport providers and streamlined the travel tickets booking across industry. INTERBUS is one of the most recognized and reputed brands in Harare, Zimbabwe.

The company’s services offers online services to transport providers to buy ticketing, advertising licenses and allows the passengers to book tickets online.INTERBUS online services are backed up by cutting edge technology, well trained man power and infrastructure to ensure the highest standards of operating efficiency. With excellent support by INTERBUS’S own state of the art technology implementations, transport provides can offer the best of the services to passengers.

Be it the management, the sales, services, the ground staff or the crew focus on performance and customer satisfaction. Every INTERBUS employee is groomed to be smart, friendly, proficient and well-informed ensuring that their interaction will make the service providers and the passengers feel welcomed and looked after.
